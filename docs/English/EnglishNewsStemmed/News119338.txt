japan's three megabank to extend loan to ntt for ntt docomo acquisit: sourc
tokyo (reuter) - japan's 3 megabank belong to mitsubishi ufj financi group 8306.t, sumitomo mitsui financi group 8316.t and mizuho financi group will be among lender to extend loan to nippon telegraph and telephon corp (ntt) 9432.t for it potenti acquisit of ntt docomo inc 9437.t, two sourc said.
