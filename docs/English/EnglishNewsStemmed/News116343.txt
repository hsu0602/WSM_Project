melvin capit rais put option on gamestop class a share to 6 million in fourth quarter 2020
(reuter) - hedg fund melvin capit manag on tuesdai disclos it had rais the put option on class a share of u.s. video game chain gamestop corp to 6 million share for the quarter end dec. 31, from 5.4 million share in the past quarter.
