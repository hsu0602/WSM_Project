stellanti scrap plan to launch peugeot in u.s. market
(reuter) - stellanti nv ha scrap a plan to launch the peugeot brand in the unit state and will focu instead on the ram pickup truck, high-margin jeep and fiat chrysler’s (fca) other brand - dodg, chrysler and alfa romeo - in the world’s second-largest auto market, it said on tuesdai.
